- No local template fallback is presented as AI content.
- Direct AI APIs fail clearly when AI config/model calls are unavailable.

- AI output never changes auction, wallet, order, settlement, cancellation, or ranking semantics.
- The saved report surface was implemented on the terminal merchant monitor rather than merchant order detail.
